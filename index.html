Top Tourist Spots in Baguio City

The Summer Capital of the Philippines

Burnham Park

A popular leisure park in the heart of Baguio City, perfect for boating, biking, and relaxing walks.

Mines View Park

Enjoy a panoramic view of Benguet’s mining town. Don’t miss the local souvenirs and photo ops with native attire!

The Mansion

The official summer residence of the Philippine President. A beautiful spot for photos and historic insight.

Wright Park

Famous for its "Pool of Pines" and horseback riding. Great for family fun and outdoor experiences.

Baguio Botanical Garden

A peaceful retreat filled with native plants, statues, and beautiful walkways showcasing local culture.
