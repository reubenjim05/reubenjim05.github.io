About the Sports Development Program

The University of the Cordilleras promotes student well-being and excellence through its Sports Development Program. It encourages holistic growth, teamwork, and leadership by offering training and competitive opportunities in a wide range of athletic disciplines.

Sports Programs Offered

  • Basketball
  • Volleyball
  • Track and Field
  • Swimming
  • Taekwondo
  • Chess
  • Table Tennis
  • Badminton

Meet the Coordinator: Reubenjim Camarao

Reubenjim Camarao is the current Sports Development Coordinator at the University of the Cordilleras. With a strong background in athletics and student leadership, he works to enhance sports engagement across the university and ensure student-athletes thrive in both academics and physical performance.
